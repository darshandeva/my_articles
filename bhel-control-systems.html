BHEL Control Systems Overview

Introduction

Bharat Heavy Electricals Limited (BHEL), a leading Indian manufacturer of power plant equipment, uses proprietary control systems for turbines and generators. These systems are optimized for integration with BHEL-manufactured equipment, ensuring seamless operation.

1. Turbine Control

BHEL employs its proprietary Turbine Supervisory and Control System (TSC), designed for steam and gas turbines.

Key Features:

  • High-Speed Governor Control: Precise speed regulation and load control with safety mechanisms like over-speed trips.
  • Vibration Monitoring: Tracks vibration levels for smooth operation.
  • Temperature Control: Manages inlet temperatures to prevent thermal stress.
  • Emergency Shutdown (ESD): Integrated trip logic for safe operation during abnormal conditions.
  • Integration with DCS: Seamless interface with Distributed Control Systems for plant control.

2. Generator Control

BHEL's Excitation Control System (ECS) and protection systems ensure stable and efficient generator operation.

Key Features:

  • Automatic Voltage Regulation (AVR): Stabilizes voltage and reactive power under varying loads.
  • Static Excitation System (SES): Fast and reliable excitation control based on power electronics.
  • Generator Protection: Comprehensive schemes for overcurrent, overvoltage, and differential protection.
  • Synchronization: Ensures precise grid synchronization.

3. Integrated Control Platform: MAX DNA

BHEL's MAX DNA platform integrates turbine, generator, and auxiliary systems into a unified architecture.

Advantages:

  • Unified control and monitoring platform.
  • Advanced diagnostics for predictive maintenance.
  • Scalability for future expansion.
  • Real-time performance optimization.
